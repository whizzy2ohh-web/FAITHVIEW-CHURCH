About FaithView

Welcome Home


At FaithView Church, we are more than just a congregation; we are a family dedicated to the principles of Reformation, Transformation, and Impact. Led by Rev. Abutu Maha, our ministry is focused on bringing the unadulterated Word of God to our generation.


We believe that when you view life through the eyes of faith, everything changes. Our services are designed to equip you with biblical truth, ignite your spirit with passionate worship, and empower you to make a tangible difference in your community.

Our Core

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To reform mindsets through the teaching of the Word, transform lives through the power of the Spirit, and impact nations with the Kingdom culture.

Our Vision

To see a global church raised in power, walking in the fullness of their identity in Christ, and establishing God's will on earth as it is in heaven.

Our Values

Word-Centered Living, Spirit-Led Worship, Excellence in Ministry, Family & Community, and Global Evangelism.

Equipping The Saints

FBI: FaithView Bible Institute

The FaithView Bible Institute (FBI) is the training arm of our church designed to deepen your theological understanding and equip you for effective ministry. Whether you are called to the pulpit or the marketplace, FBI prepares you for Impact.

Foundation School

For new believers and those seeking to understand the fundamental pillars of the Christian faith.

School of Ministry

Advanced training for workforce members, leaders, and those sensing a call to ministry.

Leadership Academy

Strategic leadership principles for church administration and marketplace dominance.
